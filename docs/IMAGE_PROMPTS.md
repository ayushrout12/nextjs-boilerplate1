# Image prompts reference

Use these prompts to regenerate or create variations of the Jasmine imagery.

**Visual theme (match auth-bg.png & hero-bg.png):** Vibrant pixel art, clean lines, saturated colors. Pink jasmine flowers (five soft pink petals, bright yellow center, green stem and leaves). Lush light green meadow with smaller white daisy-like flowers and yellow wildflowers. Rolling green hills with dense foliage, atmospheric perspective. Bright blue sky with fluffy white clouds. Peaceful, sunny, natural, inviting.

---

## Auth page background (`auth-bg.png`)

**Prompt:**
> vibrant pixel art of a serene meadow — one large pink jasmine flower (five pink petals, yellow center, green stem) in the foreground. Lush light green meadow with white daisies and yellow wildflowers. Rolling green hills, bright blue sky, fluffy white clouds. Peaceful, sunny, natural.

## Hero background (`hero-bg.png`)

**Prompt:**
> vibrant pixel art meadow landscape, hero aspect ratio. Two or more pink jasmine flowers (five pink petals, yellow center, green stems) in a lush green meadow. White daisies and yellow wildflowers scattered in grass. Rolling green hills, bright blue sky, fluffy white clouds. Same style as auth — peaceful, sunny, inviting.

## Favicon / app icon (`favicon.png`)

**Dimensions:** 32×32 or 64×64 (square)

**Prompt:**
> pixel art icon of a single pink jasmine flower (five petals, yellow center) — same style as auth/hero meadow. Simple, clean silhouette. Transparent or light green background. Vibrant, saturated colors.

## Open Graph / social share image (`og-image.png`)

**Dimensions:** 1200×630 (landscape)

**Prompt:**
> vibrant pixel art meadow banner — pink jasmine flowers, lush green meadow, white daisies, rolling hills, bright blue sky, fluffy clouds. Same style as auth/hero. Optional text: "Jasmine — The World's Best Designer" or "design anything". 16:9 aspect.

## Logo mark / sidebar icon (`logo-mark.png`)

**Dimensions:** 64×64 or 128×128 (square)

**Prompt:**
